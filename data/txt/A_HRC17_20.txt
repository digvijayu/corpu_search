		Cooperation with the United Nations, its representatives and mechanisms in the field of human rights 
		Note by the Secretariat
	In its resolution 12/2, the Human Rights Council invited the Secretary-General to submit a report to the Council at its fourteenth session and annually thereafter, in accordance with its programme of work, containing information and analysis of any available information, from all appropriate sources, on alleged reprisals against persons referred to in paragraph 1 of the resolution, as well as recommendations on how to address the issues of intimidation and reprisals. For technical reasons, the report will not be submitted to the Council at its seventeenth session, but at its eighteenth.
