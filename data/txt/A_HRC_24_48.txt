Human rights bodies and mechanisms
		Reports of the Human Rights Council Advisory Committee on its tenth and eleventh sessions
		Note by the Secretariat
1.	In accordance with paragraph 38 of the annex to Human Rights Council resolution 16/21, the annual report of the Human Rights Council Advisory Committee, consisting of the reports of the Committee on its tenth (18 to 22 February 2013) and eleventh (12 to 16 August 2013) sessions, will be considered by the Human Rights Council at its twenty-fourth session and will be the subject of an interactive dialogue with the Committee Chairperson.
2.	The report of the Advisory Committee on its tenth session is contained in document A/HRC/AC/10/3.
3.	The report of the Advisory Committee on its eleventh session, which will be contained in document A/HRC/AC/11/2, will be made available to the Human Rights Council once it has been finalized.
